My Story

From curiosity to applied AI

My path was not a straight line. It moved through curiosity, lost years, late-night rebuilding, production work, teaching, setbacks, and a renewed hunger to learn.

2015

First spark

A movie that opened a door

After watching "Who Am I", I became fascinated by computers, security, and the hidden systems behind the internet. I did not become the hacker I imagined, but those years taught me how powerful curiosity can be when it refuses to stay quiet.

2017

University

Ambition met reality

I entered Danang University of Science and Technology with big expectations, but I was not ready for the discipline that growth requires. Those years were not impressive on paper, but they became an honest baseline: talent without consistency does not compound.

2020

Rebuild

The lockdown that changed my direction

Covid isolation unexpectedly gave me focus. I discovered AI, learned Python seriously, built neural networks, and spent late nights following tutorials line by line. For the first time, I gave a technical subject my full attention.

2021

FTECH

From intern to AI engineer

I joined FTECH as an intern and grew into an AI Engineer over the following years. It was the place that gave me room to learn, ship, fail, and mature. Much of my production mindset was shaped there.

2024

Teaching

Learning by teaching

I began teaching programming to primary and secondary students. It became more than a side job: explaining Scratch, Python, and algorithms to young learners sharpened my own thinking and reminded me to work with responsibility.

2025

Transition

A difficult year, a wider world

After leaving FTECH during a layoff, I tried new companies, started freelancing on Upwork, and learned how different the market feels outside a familiar environment. It was unstable, but it widened my view of products, clients, and ownership.

2026

Mynavi

A new environment for research and growth

I joined Mynavi TechTus Vietnam as an AI Engineer, working with supportive people and gaining space to study, research, and explore current AI trends instead of being locked into a single narrow project.

2026

Graduate study

Returning to the fundamentals

I started a master's program in Mechatronics at DUT because I felt a genuine hunger for knowledge, including things I should have learned more deeply years earlier. It is late, but it is not too late.

Now

Applied AI

Helping people and businesses use AI well

Today I focus on applied AI, research, AI literacy for working professionals, productivity improvement, and digital transformation consulting for businesses. I am always open to meaningful collaboration.
